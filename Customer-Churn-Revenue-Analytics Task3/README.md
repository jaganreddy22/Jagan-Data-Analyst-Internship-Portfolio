# 📊 Customer Retention & Revenue Risk Analysis

> 🚀 A Cohort-Based Deep-Dive into Customer Churn, Retention Behavior, and Financial Risk using Power BI

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes telecom customer churn to identify **high-risk lifecycle segments**, evaluate **behavioral drivers**, and quantify **revenue exposure due to attrition**.

- 📂 Dataset Size: **7,043 customers**
- 📊 Tool Used: **Power BI + DAX**

The objective is to transform raw customer data into **actionable retention strategies**.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

- 📉 Measure Customer Churn & Retention Performance  
- 🧩 Identify High-Risk Customer Cohorts  
- 📑 Analyze Contract & Payment Behavior Impact  
- 💰 Estimate Revenue at Risk  
- 📈 Support Data-Driven Retention Decisions  

---

## 🔑 Core KPIs Defined

### 1️⃣ Customer Churn Rate  
**Formula:**  
Churned Customers ÷ Total Customers  

**Result:** 26.5%  

**Business Insight:**  
One in four customers are discontinuing service.

---

### 2️⃣ Retention Rate  
**Formula:**  
100 − Churn Rate  

**Result:** 73.5%  

**Business Insight:**  
Represents overall customer stability and loyalty.

---

### 3️⃣ Average Monthly Charges  
**Formula:**  
Average(Monthly Charges)  

**Result:** ₹64.76  

**Business Insight:**  
Indicates recurring revenue contribution per customer.

---

### 4️⃣ Average Customer Lifetime Value (Proxy)  
**Formula:**  
Average(Total Charges)  

**Result:** ₹2,279  

**Business Insight:**  
Estimated long-term revenue per customer.

---

### 5️⃣ Revenue at Risk  
**Formula:**  
Churned Customers × Avg Monthly Charges  

**Result:** ₹1.21 Lakhs  

**Business Insight:**  
Quantifies monthly revenue exposure due to churn.

---

## 🔎 Deep-Dive Analysis

### 🧭 Cohort-Based Retention Analysis

Customers were segmented into lifecycle cohorts based on tenure:

- 🟢 0–12 Months  
- 🟡 12–24 Months  
- 🔵 24–48 Months  
- 🟣 48+ Months  

📌 **Key Finding:**  
Nearly **48% of churn occurs within the first 12 months**, indicating critical early-stage risk.

---

### 📑 Contract-Based Risk Analysis

- 🔴 Month-to-month contracts: **43% churn**
- 🟡 One-year contracts: **11% churn**
- 🟢 Two-year contracts: **2.8% churn**

📌 **Insight:**  
Long-term contracts significantly improve retention.

---

### 💳 Payment Behavior Analysis

Electronic check users show the highest churn rate (**45%**).

📌 **Insight:**  
Payment method may correlate with customer dissatisfaction or instability.

---

## 💰 Financial Impact Assessment

Churned customers generate:

- Higher monthly charges  
- Lower overall lifetime value  

📉 **Estimated Revenue at Risk:** ₹1.21 Lakhs per month

This highlights the urgency of retention-focused strategies.
